'I don't know why we're doing it' - Americans divided on Iran war

It has been 10 days since the US and Israel first launched an attack on Iran on 28 February.

In a wide-ranging press conference from Mar-a-Lago on Tuesday, US President Donald Trump said the war would be over 'very soon', but is not over yet. Trump added that he wants to ensure Iran cannot develop nuclear weaponry "for a very long period of time".

The BBC asked Americans from across the US about their take on the war and what the best outcome could be.
